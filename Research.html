Primary Reseach

This is a screenshot of the homepage on the Hilton Pond website. I've included it in my research because it shows the logo, page background and basic page layout for their entire website. Everything follows this style and use of Comic Sans.

Left & Right

This image is a screenshot of Hilton Pond Center's main page, just after the header information and navigation. It shows the important news for the week, current updates, and button for their weekly newsletter that points to a different box about humming birds. Everything commands attention and equal importance because everything is brightly colored or bolded in some way. This is pretty typically information one might find on a homepage, however it is very hard to visually sift through to find what you are looking for.

This screenshot is of the newsletter sign-up page. When you click "Weekly Happenings" on the homepage, it takes you to the newletter sign up page, not the actual newsletter. The newsletter is located on a different page entirely and I only found that by accident. This page includes a different logo that could be considered to be on brand with the rest of the website, however it feels more like an accident then on purpose.

Images associated with Hilton Pond Center
