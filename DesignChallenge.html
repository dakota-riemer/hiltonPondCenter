Design Challenge

What is the Problem You are Trying to Solve?

To make Hilton Pond Center's branding clear and modern so that they will be able to attract more people to their cause.

Take a Stab at Framing it as a Design Question

How can I make make Hilton Pond Center's branding clear and modern so that they will be able to attract more people to their cause?

Now, State the Ultimate Impact You are Trying to Have.

I want the Hilton Pond Center to be able to reach its goals and continue its research through a better designed platform.

Finally, Write Down Some of the Context and Constraints You are Facing.

There organization has been established for over twenty years and the majority of it's audience is over 50 years old. They may not be welcome to change.

Does Your Original Question Need a Tweek? Try it again.

How can I improve the design of Hilton Pond Center's website to help them reach their goals and continue their research with better foot traffic to their website?
